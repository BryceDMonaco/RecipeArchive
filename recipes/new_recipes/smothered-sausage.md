Smothered Sausage

Ingredients:
- 1 yellow onion sliced very fine
- 1/2 of a 28 oz can of San Marzano tomatoes, chopped
- 1 yellow or red bell pepper, skinned and julienned
- 1 lb mild italian sausage links
- Olive oil
- Salt
- Pepper

Steps:
1. Add olive oil and sliced onions to a large pan, covered, with medium heat. Allow the onion to wilt and reduce, then uncover the pan, raise heat to medium-high, and cook the onion to a deep golden brown.
2. Add the tomatoes and their juice, salt, and pepper, then lower the heat to medium low to gently simmer, uncovered, for 20 minutes.
3. Add the bell pepper strips and the sausage, making room to nestle the sausage into thr eauce and veggies. Puncture the sausage skin with a fork in a few places. Cover the pan and allow to cook for 20 minutes, turning the sausages and veggies occasionally.

Source:

Modified from an original recipe by Marcella Hazan, *Essentials of classic Italaian Cooking*

Notes:

We like to use two bell peppers of different colors and an entire 28oz can of tomatoes

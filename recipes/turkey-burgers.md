## Ingredients

- At least 1 lb ground turkey
- 1/4 cup chia seeds per lb of ground turkey
- Spices to taste

## Instructions

1. In a bowl mix together ground turkey, chia seeds, and spices.
2. Place parchment paper on one or multiple baking sheets, depending on how many patties you are making.
3. Divide mixture into 3 balls per lb of turkey, placing each ball onto the lined baking sheet(s).
4. Squish patties to match the size of your buns.
5. Cover each sheet with plastic wrap and put the sheets in the refrigerator for 30 minutes.
6. Patties can now be cooked, stored in the fridge, or frozen. If storing, place a piece of parchment paper between each patty so they do not freeze stuck together.

### Cooking Instructions (Thawed)

#### Grill/Barbeque
7. Heat grill to 400, put patties on grill, cook for eight minutes, flip, cook for another eight minutes (add cheese around 1:30 left).

#### Oven
8. Preheat oven to 375, put patties on foil lined baking sheet, bake for 30 minutes (add cheese at 2 minutes left).

## Ingredients

- 2 Tbsp butter
- 2 Tbsp olive oil
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp sugar
- 1 tsp oregano
- 1 tsp red pepper flakes
- 1 tsp basil
- 1/2 tsp MSG
- 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
- 1/4 tsp garlic powder
- 1/4 tsp onion powder
- 1 bay leaf
- 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
- 1 can (6 oz) tomato paste

## Instructions

1. Melt the butter and olive oil together in a saucepan.
2. Add the salt, sugar, oregano, red pepper flakes, basil, MSG, cayenne pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and bay leaf to the pan.
3. Mix the seasonings together with the butter and oil.
4. Add the tomato sauce and tomato paste to the pan.
5. Mix everything together, then remove from heat and serve.

title: Homemade German Spaetzle

## Ingredients

- 4 eggs
- 1/3 cup milk (add more if batter is too dry)
- 2 teaspoons salt
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
- 1 teaspoon white pepper
- 1/4 teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley (optional garnish)

## Instructions

1. Whisk together eggs, milk, and salt in a medium bowl. Stir in flour until well combined. Adjust consistency by adding more flour if too runny or more milk if too thick.
2. Bring a large pot of salted water to a rapid boil. Position your spaetzle maker over the pot and pour half the batter into the cup. Slide the cup back and forth quickly to drop batter through. Repeat with remaining batter, working fast until all batter cooks in the water.
3. Melt butter in a separate pan and season with white pepper and nutmeg. Once spaetzle finishes cooking and rises to the surface, drain and toss with the seasoned butter. Garnish with parsley if desired.

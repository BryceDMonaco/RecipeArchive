title: Baked Ziti with Sausage

## Ingredients

- 1 lb ziti noodles
- 1 1/2 lbs ground spicy or sweet Italian sausage
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 (28-oz) can crushed tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 1/2 teaspoons sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 1/3 cup plus 3 tablespoons grated pecorino Romano (or Parmigiano Reggiano), divided
- 1/3 cup chopped fresh basil, plus more for serving
- 8 oz whole milk mozzarella cheese, shredded (about 2 cups)

## Instructions

1. Boil a large pot of salted water and add ziti noodles. Cook according to package directions until very al dente (about 7 minutes). Drain and return to pot.
2. Preheat oven to 425°F with rack in middle position.
3. Heat a large saute pan over medium-high heat. Crumble sausage into pan and cook, breaking it apart, for 5-6 minutes until lightly browned. Transfer to a plate with a slotted spoon. Drain all but 1 tablespoon fat (add olive oil if needed).
4. Over low heat, add minced garlic and cook for about 1 minute until soft. Stir in crushed tomatoes, salt, sugar, and red pepper flakes. Simmer uncovered for 10 minutes.
5. Add cream, 1/3 cup pecorino Romano, cooked sausage, and basil to the pan. Combine thoroughly.
6. Pour sauce mixture into pot with pasta and stir gently. Transfer half the mixture to a 9x13-inch baking dish. Layer with half the mozzarella and half the remaining pecorino Romano.
7. Top with remaining pasta mixture, then remaining mozzarella and pecorino Romano.
8. Bake uncovered for 15-20 minutes until cheese melts and browns. Garnish with additional basil before serving.

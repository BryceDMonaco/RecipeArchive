title: Tarkenian Night Flower Cocktail
tags: cocktail
---

## Ingredients

- 1/2 oz Gin
- 1/2 oz Elderflower Liqueur (St Germain's)
- 3/4 oz Huckleberry Syrup
- 1 oz Lava Mule Mix (Ginger & Lime)
- Club Soda (1+ oz to fill, no more than 2 oz)
- Candied Hibiscus Flower (Optional Garnish)

## Instructions

1. Combine all ingredients except club soda and shake with ice.
2. Strain into 8 oz glass filled fully with crushed ice.
3. Fill with club soda.
4. Garnish with a candied hibiscus flower on top of the ice.

## Notes

Huckleberry cocktail inspired by Olga's Cantina in Galaxy's Edge.

title: One-Pot Lemon Garlic Shrimp Pasta
tags: pasta, seafood, one-pot, quick
---

## Ingredients

- 16 oz linguine
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 4 tablespoons unsalted butter (1/2 stick)
- 4 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
- 1 1/4 lb large shrimp
- Salt, to taste
- Pepper, to taste
- 1 teaspoon dried oregano
- 4 cups baby spinach
- 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated
- 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice

## Instructions

1. Boil water in a large pot and add pasta. Cook, stirring frequently, until al dente.
2. Drain and set the cooked pasta aside.
3. In the same pan, heat olive oil and 2 tablespoons of butter. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes, cooking until fragrant.
4. Add shrimp, salt, and pepper, stirring until shrimp begin turning pink but aren't fully cooked.
5. Stir in oregano and spinach, cooking until the spinach wilts.
6. Return pasta to the pot with remaining butter, parmesan, and parsley. Mix thoroughly until butter melts.
7. Once shrimp are fully cooked, add lemon juice and mix once more.
8. Serve while hot.

## Notes

Modified to use half the amount of butter, double the amount of pasta.

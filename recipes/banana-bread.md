## Ingredients

- 3 ripe bananas, mashed
- 1/3 cup melted butter
- 3/4 cup sugar
- 1 large egg, beaten
- 1 tsp vanilla extract
- 1 tsp baking soda
- Pinch of salt
- 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
- Optional: 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or chocolate chips

## Instructions

1. Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C).
2. Grease a 9x5 inch loaf pan.
3. In a large bowl, mash bananas with a fork.
4. Mix in melted butter.
5. Stir in sugar, egg, and vanilla extract.
6. Sprinkle baking soda and salt over the mixture and mix in.
7. Add flour and stir until just combined. Don't overmix.
8. Fold in nuts or chocolate chips if using.
9. Pour batter into prepared loaf pan.
10. Bake for 60-65 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in center comes out clean.
11. Cool in pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto wire rack.

## Notes

- The blacker the bananas, the better! Very ripe bananas give the best flavor.
- Don't overmix the batter or the bread will be tough.
- Freezes well for up to 3 months.

## Ingredients

- 1 yellow or white onion, chopped
- 2 celery stalks, chopped
- 1 jalapeno, chopped
- 3-4 cloves garlic, chopped
- 8 oz mushrooms, sliced (optional)
- 2 15 oz cans tomato sauce
- 2 28 oz cans San Marzano tomatoes
- 2 12 oz cans tomato paste
- 3 cups water
- 1 tsp onion powder
- 3 tsp garlic powder
- 3 tsp italian seasoning
- 3 tsp parsley flakes
- 1 tsp sugar (optional)
- 1/4 cup red wine
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes (more to taste)
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large pot, cook onion, celery, and jalapeno in olive oil until limp, add garlic and mushrooms about halfway through.
2. Add tomatoes, sauce, paste, and water. Cook to a slow boil.
3. Add garlic powder, onion powder, italian seasoning, parsley, red pepper flakes, sugar, salt, pepper, and red wine.
4. Cook at a slow boil for at least 4 to 5 hours, stirring somewhat frequently, especially later.

## Notes

This makes enough sauce for 4 to 5 lbs of pasta and freezes well.

Meat Sauce: Add 1 1/2 lb ground beef, browned and drained.

Mushroom Sauce: Add an additional 1 lb sliced, cooked mushrooms.

Sausage Sauce: Add 2 lb cooked sausage + fat, cut if links.

Vegetable Sauce: Add zucchini, summer squash, asparagus, broccoli, eggplant, cauliflower, peas, beans, carrots, spinach, or any other veggies.

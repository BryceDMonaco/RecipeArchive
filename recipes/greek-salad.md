## Ingredients

- 4 cups chopped romaine lettuce
- 2 large tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 cucumber, sliced
- 1 red onion, thinly sliced
- 1 cup Kalamata olives
- 1 cup crumbled feta cheese
- 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
- 2 tbsp red wine vinegar
- 1 tsp dried oregano
- Salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. In a large bowl, combine lettuce, tomatoes, cucumber, and red onion.
2. Add Kalamata olives and feta cheese.
3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, red wine vinegar, oregano, salt, and pepper.
4. Pour dressing over salad and toss gently to combine.
5. Serve immediately.

## Notes

- For best flavor, let the salad sit for 10 minutes before serving to allow flavors to meld.
- Can be made ahead without dressing and stored in refrigerator.
- Add grilled chicken for a complete meal.
